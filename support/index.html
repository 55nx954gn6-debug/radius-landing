Radius support

How can we help?

For app support, pilot questions, community onboarding, safety reports, or account requests, email Radius support.

Email support Start a pilot

Common support topics

  • Account help: login, password reset, profile updates, or account deletion requests.
  • Community access: joining a community, pilot setup, QR codes, or branded onboarding.
  • Safety: reporting content, blocking or muting another user, or escalating urgent concerns.
  • App issues: feed, Signals, messages, notifications, calendar, location, camera, or photo access.

Safety and moderation

You can report Signals or user behavior in the Radius app where reporting controls are available. For additional help, include the relevant community, Signal, username/handle, and a short description when emailing support.

Response expectations

Radius is currently onboarding selected pilot communities. We review support requests as quickly as practical, with safety and account-access issues prioritized.
