Current product direction

Signals are the lightweight language of “who’s in?”

Members can post what they are doing, where help is needed, or what is happening soon. Radius keeps the surface intentionally local, timely, and human-scale.

Community SignalsServe projects, family picnics, walks, pickup games, coffee, board games, small groups, and local meetups.
Goodwill requestsPractical “could someone help?” moments routed through trusted community context instead of awkward broadcast texts.
Event timingSignals can carry dates, expiration windows, attendee state, and calendar handoff when the moment needs structure.
Feed + map discoveryBrowse community or nearby Signals with radius controls, search, and map/list surfaces.

Designed for trust

Belonging without public virality.

Radius is built around bounded communities, approximate location, intentional visibility, reporting, block/mute controls, and support paths. The point is presence, not engagement theater.
