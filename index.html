What Radius does today

Post a Signal. Set the scope. Let the right people find it.

Signals are lightweight posts for events, meetups, needs, and local intent. Members can browse by community or all nearby Signals, search, switch list/map surfaces, and respond without digging through announcement sprawl.

01

Approximate by default

Radius is designed around general area discovery, not exact public tracking. Location sharing is intentional, scoped, and can stay locationless when a Signal does not need a place.

02

Bounded communities

Communities can carry their own branded onboarding, splash identity, join flow, and feed context — useful for churches, campuses, clubs, and local groups.

03

Safety controls

Radius includes signal reporting, block/mute relationships, admin report routing, and support paths so pilots do not depend on vibes alone.
